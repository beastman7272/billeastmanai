About Bill

I've spent my career making technology useful for people who don't have time to chase it.

I spent a decade leading data science and AI teams at AT&T — building the kinds of systems that most small businesses never get access to. Now I help smaller organizations close that gap.

After years working at scale — real-time machine learning, generative AI applications, analytics platforms used across a Fortune 10 company — I've seen what these tools can actually do when they're implemented well. Most of what's written about AI for business isn't built for organizations without a dedicated IT team or a six-figure software budget. That's the problem I'm here to solve.

I work with small businesses, nonprofits, and local organizations that suspect AI could help but don't know where to start. I help bring you up to speed on the tools that can address your needs and bring you real results.

What I bring
